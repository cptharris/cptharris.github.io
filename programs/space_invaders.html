Space Invaders

Aliens are coming to invade your planet! Place your mouse over them to shoot them down.

Oh no! As you shoot down more aliens, they get angry and start sending in more aliens to attack your planet. On top of that, their ships seem to get faster! Watch out at higher levels!

Unfortunately, your planet can only hold out for so long. You have 40 lives (2 per each red ball). When you have no more lives, your planet falls.

Defend your planet!
